Teach-A-Robot: Teaching Robots Through Human Demonstrations

Motivation

In this HIT, you will be asked to complete a series of goals, each of which involves moving a robot through a simulated world to pick up and place various objects. How you choose to grasp the objects and move the robot will be recorded and used to teach robots to perform similar tasks faster and better. Detailed instructions on how to use the simulator are provided before you begin the demonstration.

You will be given 75 minutes to complete and submit your progress. This should give you a comfortable amount of time to complete the given task.

Requirements

You must use a WebGL-enabled web-browser; we recommend Google Chrome.

Prior video-game experience can be helpful.

Reward Policy

As long as you successfully complete the first goal (i.e. picking up the first object), your results will be accepted. We encourage you to continue the task through added monetary rewards.

Rewards will be given based upon how many goals were completed in a given demonstration:

  • Each goal is worth $0.50.
  • An additional $1.00 will be given for full completion of all 12 goals in the task.
  • Since there are 12 goals in this task, the maximum payout is $7.00 for the full completion of this HIT.

If you choose a grasp for the robot to hold an object too unrealistically, you will not recieve payment for that goal.
